"We Are Jadara Environmental Solutions and Organic Waste & Garbage Treatment LLC"

Jadara Environmental Solutions is registered under the companies act and related laws of the Dubai Municipality government. We are an Environmental Waste Management & Treatment company on a mission to create a sustainable environment through consultations, environmental assessments & audits, waste recycling, disinfection and sterilization for infected and uninfected areas, Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA), air assessment, Environmental Management System (ISO 14001:2015), environmental training courses, partnerships, and collaboration.
